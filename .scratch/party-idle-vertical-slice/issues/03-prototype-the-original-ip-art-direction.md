# Prototype the original-IP art direction

Type: prototype
Status: resolved
Blocked by: none

## Question

What original visual language gives the four Classes, opponents, Stage backdrops, and effects a bright chibi fantasy appeal while remaining recognizably distinct from Ragnarok Online and readable at the compact Battlefield's native scale?

## Comments

Three original visual-language directions are ready for live review in the
[art-direction prototype](../../../prototype/art-direction/README.md):
Sunsteel Caravan, Moonberry Guild, and Skyglass Company. Each is exercised in
the exact 480×112 Battle Tile with 32×48-at-1× Party Members, five opponents,
separate effects, a Stage backdrop, and an enlarged four-Class silhouette board.

This HITL ticket remained claimed until the user's selection was recorded below.

## Answer

Use **Moonberry Guild** as the vertical slice's original-IP visual language.
It combines storybook night-garden fantasy with plum contour lines,
mint/berry/cream color families, plump leaf and stitched silhouettes,
orchard-like Stage backdrops, whimsical botanical opponents, and luminous
petal/halo Ability effects. This gives the four Classes a cohesive bright chibi
world without borrowing Ragnarok Online's specific costume, monster, place,
lore, or Ability identity.

Sunsteel Caravan was also acceptable, but it does not remain a parallel style.
Carry forward only its bold-contour and warm-contrast discipline as a
readability gate for Moonberry assets at the native 32×48-at-1× scale. Reject
Skyglass Company's crystalline magitech language for this vertical slice.
Character bodies and Ability effects remain separate assets, and every future
ComfyUI or AutoSprite proof must be judged inside the fixed 480×112 Battle Tile
against the five-opponent stress case.

See the [prototype verdict and retained reference](../../../prototype/art-direction/NOTES.md).
