# Define the four Class kits

Type: grilling
Status: resolved
Blocked by: 06

## Question

What distinct combat identity, Ability pool, Ability Loadout constraints, and respeccable Talent Tree should Knight, Wizard, Priest, and Hunter each have in the vertical slice without creating one mandatory Party composition?

## Answer

- Shared Class Kit structure: one fixed basic attack, four Core Abilities available immediately, a three-slot Ability Loadout, and one Talent Tier. Its Stat Row offers two five-rank Stat Talents but accepts at most five points distributed freely between them. Its Ability Row then accepts the sixth point in one of two mutually exclusive Ability Talents.
- Any unlocked Core Ability or Ability Talent may occupy any of the three Ability Loadout slots. There are no reserved, mandatory, or Class-specific slot rules; the fixed basic attack remains outside the loadout as its fallback.
- Every possible three-Class Party is a supported clear-capable composition with suitable loadouts, Talents, and reasonable Equipment, though compositions need not clear at equal speed. Stages cannot require a particular Class, Healing, Stun, damage channel, or other Class-exclusive answer. Knight and Priest improve protection and sustain respectively but are not mandatory, and every Class provides a secondary contribution beyond its primary identity.
- Class identities: Knight provides frontline durability and Party protection with secondary Physical Damage and Stun; Wizard provides burst and multi-target Elemental Damage with secondary defensive magic; Priest provides Healing, revival, and Buffs with secondary single-target Elemental Damage; Hunter provides sustained single-target Physical Damage with secondary Armor reduction and Stun.
- The vertical slice retains Physical Damage and Elemental Damage as its two mechanical channels. Elemental Damage replaces the earlier `Magic Damage` term; individual Elemental Resistance values and randomized Equipment interactions remain later extensions.
- Physical Power and Elemental Power are hidden derived statistics: `floor((base value + flat bonuses) × (1 + summed percentage bonuses))`. An Ability applies its authored coefficient to the appropriate Power. The interface hides the consolidated Power totals but shows each Ability's current raw Damage result.
- Healing uses an Ability coefficient of Elemental Power rather than a third offensive or Healing-specific statistic.
- Level increases do not automatically raise combat statistics in the vertical slice. Each Level's power increase comes from its granted Talent Point and from Equipment progression.
- Level 1 bases are: Knight `180 Health / 14 Physical / 4 Elemental / 30 Armor / 12 Elemental Resistance`; Wizard `100 / 4 / 16 / 10 / 24`; Priest `125 / 5 / 13 / 15 / 20`; Hunter `120 / 15 / 6 / 16 / 14`.
- Knight basic `Steel Cut`: closest opponent, 100% Physical, 0.35s Wind-up, 0.65s Recovery. Core Abilities: `Sweeping Arc` (all opponents, 70% Physical, 0.50s/0.70s, 6s cooldown); `Shield Brace` (self `Braced`, +50 Armor for 5s, valid while absent, 0.10s/0.40s, 9s); `Rallying Guard` (Party `Guarded`, +15 Armor and Elemental Resistance for 6s, valid while absent, 0.30s/0.50s, 12s); `Pommel Break` (closest, 90% Physical plus 1.2s Stun, 0.25s/0.65s, 9s). Default loadout: Shield Brace, Rallying Guard, Sweeping Arc.
- Knight Stat Row: `Fortitude` (+6% Max Health per rank) and `Swordcraft` (+5% Physical Power per rank). Ability Row: `Hold the Line` (valid below 50% Health; self +60 Armor and +30 Elemental Resistance for 6s; 0.20s/0.50s, 15s) or `Falling Star` (all opponents, 150% Physical plus 1s Stun; 0.70s/0.80s, 12s; Bosses ignore Stun).
- Wizard basic `Arc Spark`: closest opponent, 100% Lightning Elemental, 0.45s/0.75s. Core Abilities: `Cinder Bloom` (all opponents, 80% Fire Elemental, 0.60s/0.70s, 7s); `Frost Lance` (closest, 180% Frost Elemental, 0.80s/0.60s, 8s); `Prism Ward` (Party `Warded`, +20 Elemental Resistance for 6s, valid while absent, 0.30s/0.50s, 12s); `Thunder Ring` (all opponents, 60% Lightning Elemental plus 1s Stun, 0.50s/0.80s, 11s). Default loadout: Prism Ward, Frost Lance, Cinder Bloom.
- Wizard Stat Row: `Elemental Practice` (+5% Elemental Power per rank) and `Warding Lore` (+4 flat Elemental Resistance per rank). Ability Row: `Starfall` (all opponents, 160% Fire Elemental; 0.90s/0.80s, 14s) or `Prismatic Shelter` (Party `Sheltered`, +25 Armor and +30 Elemental Resistance for 6s; 0.40s/0.60s, 15s).
- Priest basic `Sun Mote`: closest opponent, 100% Light Elemental, 0.50s/0.80s. Core Abilities: `Mending Light` (lowest-health living Party Member missing Health, 140% Elemental Power as Healing, 0.45s/0.55s, 5s); `Dawn Recall` (first Knocked Out Party Member by Formation order, revive with Health equal to 200% Elemental Power, 1.20s/0.80s, 20s); `War Hymn` (Party `Inspired`, +20% Physical and Elemental Power for 8s, valid while absent, 0.40s/0.60s, 14s); `Judgment` (closest, 170% Light Elemental, 0.70s/0.60s, 7s). Default loadout: Dawn Recall, Mending Light, War Hymn.
- Priest Stat Row: `Devotion` (+5% Max Health per rank) and `Radiant Study` (+5% Elemental Power per rank). Ability Row: `Moonwell` (all living Party Members, 100% Elemental Power as Healing, valid while anyone is missing Health, 0.80s/0.70s, 12s) or `Sunlance` (closest, 250% Light Elemental and `Exposed` for 6s, reducing Armor and Elemental Resistance by 20; 0.80s/0.70s, 12s).
- Hunter basic `Quickshot`: closest opponent, 100% Physical, 0.30s/0.55s. Core Abilities: `Pinpoint Shot` (closest, 190% Physical, 0.65s/0.55s, 7s); `Barbed Arrow` (closest, 90% Physical and `Riven`, -20 Armor for 6s, valid while absent, 0.40s/0.55s, 10s); `Split Volley` (all opponents, 75% Physical, 0.55s/0.65s, 7s); `Snareburst` (all opponents, 55% Physical plus 1.2s Stun, 0.45s/0.70s, 12s). Default loadout: Barbed Arrow, Pinpoint Shot, Split Volley.
- Hunter Stat Row: `Draw Weight` (+5% Physical Power per rank) and `Fieldcraft` (+4 flat Armor per rank). Ability Row: `Heartseeker` (closest, 280% Physical; 0.85s/0.65s, 13s) or `Moonwire Trap` (all opponents, 80% Physical plus 2s Stun; 0.65s/0.75s, 15s; Bosses ignore Stun).
- Talent respec is free but manual, one point at a time. The Stat Row accepts at most five points; after five total points it unlocks the Ability Row, which accepts one point in either Ability Talent. The Ability Talent must be removed before the Stat Row can fall below five points. Talent edits made during combat apply at the next Wave or Boss boundary. A removed Ability Talent is removed from its loadout slot; a newly unlocked Ability inserted into a loadout receives the normal full-cooldown Activation Delay, which is the only switching penalty.
- Numeric order is: `Power = floor((base + flat bonuses) × (1 + summed percentage bonuses))`; `raw Damage or Healing = floor(Power × Ability coefficient)`. After reductions, Armor and Elemental Resistance are clamped to a minimum of zero before the existing mitigation formula. Healing uses its raw result directly. Ability descriptions show current raw results before target mitigation, while consolidated Power totals remain hidden.
